My Priorities

As your representative for Westbourne & Poets' Corner, I'll fight for:

1. Direct Democracy

You deserve a direct say in local decisions. I will:

  • Host regular community forums on major local issues
  • Create online platforms for resident feedback

  • Hold the council accountable to residents' wishes
  • Fight against decisions made without proper consultation
