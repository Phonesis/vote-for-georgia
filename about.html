About Georgia

Georgia McKinley Fitch is your independent candidate for the Westbourne & Poets' Corner ward by-election on May 1st. She believes in empowering you to have a direct say in local issues that affect your daily life.

As a member of the Independents for Direct Democracy, Georgia is committed to:

  • Ensuring residents have a direct say in local decisions via referendums
  • Holding the council accountable for their actions
  • Fighting for transparency in local government
  • Standing up against party politics that don't serve local needs

Georgia's vision for the ward

Georgia's youthful passion and rigour give her the mindset and energy needed to navigate council bureaucracy and get things done for your ward.

If elected, Georgia promises to hold regular surgeries, be accessible to all constituents, and provide regular updates on council activities that affect your area.

Georgia believes that local government should be transparent, responsive, and focused on delivering high-quality services that meet the needs of all residents. Her vision for our community includes:

  • Direct Democracy enshrined into the local council's constitution
  • A cleaner, greener environment with well-maintained parks and public spaces
  • Safe streets with effective policing and community support
  • Action on pot holes and sagging infrastructure within the ward

Georgia stands for practical, common-sense solutions that make a real difference to people's lives. She believes in listening to residents and working collaboratively to address local issues.
