My Priorities

Your Direct Democracy Candidate

Georgia is a lifelong resident of Brighton & Hove who understands the issues that matter to those in the Westbourne & Poets' Corner ward. She has lived and worked in the ward and as a result knows the challenges faced by business owners and local residents.

As your councillor, Georgia will be accessible, accountable and work tirelessly to represent your interests at the council.

Georgia is a member of the Independents for Direct Democracy party, which is committed to giving you a direct say in local issues that affect your daily life.


'I am deeply committed to our community and have consistently demonstrated resilience and determination. This marks the third local election in which I have stood as a candidate, and I remain unwavering in my dedication to serve the community in which I was raised' -Georgia
